CRIMINAL INCIDENT RESPONSE PROTOCOL v1.0

PURPOSE
To establish rapid and coordinated response procedures for criminal incidents affecting organizational assets, personnel, or operations.

SCOPE
All employees and contractors must adhere to this protocol for any criminal incident discovery or reporting.

INCIDENT RESPONSE FRAMEWORK

1. IMMEDIATE RESPONSE (0-2 HOURS)
1.1 Upon discovery of criminal incident:
    - Evacuate affected area if safety risk present
    - Preserve evidence and scene integrity
    - Contact local law enforcement immediately
    - Document initial observations without contaminating evidence

1.2 Notify incident response team leader and management chain:
    - Provide incident classification (fraud, theft, cybercrime, violence, etc.)
    - Specify location and affected parties
    - Report estimated impact scope

1.3 Secure all physical evidence:
    - Do not move or handle evidence unnecessarily
    - Establish perimeter to prevent unauthorized access
    - Assign evidence custodian for continuity

2. INVESTIGATION COORDINATION (2-24 HOURS)
2.1 Establish incident command post with:
    - Police liaison
    - Internal legal representative
    - Incident coordinator
    - Relevant department heads

2.2 Conduct preliminary investigation:
    - Interview initial witnesses (documented with written consent)
    - Photograph scenes and evidence
    - Secure digital evidence and systems
    - Prepare case package for law enforcement

2.3 Victim support:
    - Medical assistance arranged if needed
    - Psychological support resources offered
    - Communication protocol established

3. DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
3.1 All incident communications logged with timestamps.
3.2 Witness statements recorded (audio preferred, transcribed within 24 hours).
3.3 Evidence inventory maintained with photographic documentation.
3.4 External communications logged including police case numbers.

4. FOLLOW-UP COORDINATION
4.1 Designated liaison assigned to law enforcement for ongoing coordination.
4.2 Regular updates provided to affected stakeholders within legal confidentiality limits.
4.3 Internal reports prepared for management and compliance review.

LEGAL FRAMEWORK
This protocol ensures compliance with Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023 procedural requirements and cooperation with investigating authorities.
